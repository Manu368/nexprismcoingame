How to Play (Rule)

The strategy of the game is as follows: You always bet only on one of any sides. Let's take the Heads side for example. If you win, you must bet the minimum bet. If you lose, double your bet until you win. After you win, you need to return the bet again. And change the coin to "Tail". With this approach, in any case, you will be in the black. In fact, everything is quite simple.
